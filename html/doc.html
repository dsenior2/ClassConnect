Introduction

ClassConnect will give students a centralized location to communicate with students in their classes, form study groups, and get reviews and information about the contents of a specific course. Students will be able to find peers in their class and form study group-chats using the messaging feature on the website. Students can also interact with each other by creating general posts on the course’s discussion board. ClassConnect will also include a description of the class and reviews from students who previously took the class. Users will be able to rate these reviews, and if a review is rated poorly it will be removed. Overall, ClassConnect will combine the features students use on Webcourses, Facebook, and GroupMe in one centralized location, in an easy to use format.

A Word of Advice

Before you go to see your new awesome theme, here are few tips on how to familiarize yourself with it:

  • ClassConnect is based on Bootstrap 3. If you are unfamiliar with Bootstrap, visit their website and read through the documentation. All of Bootstrap components have been modified to fit the style of AdminLTE and provide a consistent look throughout the template. This way, we guarantee you will get the best of AdminLTE.
  • Documentation. We are trying our best to make your experience with AdminLTE be smooth. One way to achieve that is to provide documentation and support. If you think that something is missing from the documentation, please do not hesitate to create an issue to tell us about it. Also, if you would like to contribute, email the support team at support@almsaeedstudio.com.
  • Built with LESS. This theme uses the LESS compiler to make it easier to customize and use. LESS is easy to learn if you know CSS or SASS. It is not necessary to learn LESS but it will benefit you a lot in the future.

Note: LESS files are better commented than the compiled CSS file.
